The Story

Twelve warnings. One left.

Detective Mara Vance has spent six months tracking a killer who never strikes without warning. Each crime is preceded by an anonymous message — a cryptic line, a countdown, a name. Twelve messages. Twelve victims. Every prediction, exact.

Then the thirteenth message arrives. But this time the name on the screen is her own. With seventy-two hours on the clock and a department that no longer trusts her, Mara must decode a pattern that seems to know her better than she knows herself — before the final message becomes a confession written in her blood.

Some messages predict the future. This one was meant to end it.
